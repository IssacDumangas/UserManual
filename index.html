Website User Manual

          In this manual, all of the feature, and how-to-do-what will be fully explained and disclosed so that any future and would be user of the website will remain informed about the steps and the limitations of the website, making it easier and as user friendly as possible. Terminologies will be further elaborated as well.

Landing Page

          The Landing Page is the first interface the website will show to anyone who searches for it regardless of privileges, the landing page shows the same exact information to both admin and user. The Landing page is also divided into three sections, The Title, About, and History. The Title is where the logo and the main subject of the website are located, it lets the user know about what the website is about but not enough information to for people outside the organization to know what’s inside. The About section is for the organization lodge details and how it became as an organization it fully details the essentially the “details” of this specific lodge. In The History on the other hand contains the general history of masonry internationally and in how it reached the philippines.

Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is the main navigation tool that would help the user navigate the part of the website to direct them to where they want to be.

1 Home - in the landing page, clicking Home will scroll up the page if the user happens to be at the bottom part of the website.

2 About - Clicking the “About” in the navigation bar will automatically scroll down the page to the “About” section of the page.

3 History - Clicking “History” on the navigation bar will scroll the page in the history part of the website.

4 Events - Clicking “Events” will open a new page that would show events that the organization allowed to be seen by outsiders.

5 News - Clicking on the “News” will open a new page that would show news that the organization allowed to be seen by outsiders.

6 Activities - Clicking on the “Activities” will open a new page that would show activities that the organization allowed to be seen by outsiders.

7 Post Content - Clicking on the “Post Content” will open a new page that would show Posts that the organization shared publicly.

8 Officers - Clicking on the “Officers” will introduce users to the officers in charge of the organization.

9 Login - Clicking on the Login will direct the user to the Login page if he or she decided to login.

Login

          To login is very simple, just click “Login” at the top right part of the page in the navigation bar Input your username and password, do note that the website will automatically detect if your credentials are for admin or for standard user. After inputting all the information, click “login” to continue to the website.
